We're excited to announce that Slang will have a strong presence at **[SIGGRAPH 2026](https://s2026.siggraph.org/)** in Los Angeles this July! This year's conference features three courses covering Slang and neural shading techniques, a Birds of a Feather session for community discussion and updates, and several additional community events.

Whether you're new to shader programming, building neural graphics workflows, or curious about where the shading ecosystem is headed, there's a session for you.

Visit our [SIGGRAPH 2026 landing page](https://shader-slang.org/landing/siggraph-26/) for the full schedule, session descriptions, and links to materials as they become available.

We hope to see you in Los Angeles!
